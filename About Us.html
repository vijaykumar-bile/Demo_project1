About InDeft

InDeft Technology Solutions is a game-changer in the Information Technology space with 4+years of distinctive expertise in IT-Infrastructure, Application development, and Automation. Our experienced approach focuses on Digital IT, a global expertise in Solution Design, Integrity, Project Management, Technical Expertise, Intellectual Property, Partnerships. Our ability to seamlessly orchestrate transformations and implement automation has positioned us as an emerging IT provider.

We have succeeded in developing models and tools that serve to identify our client’s specific requirements and cater to addressing them in the most effective and efficient manner. We firmly believe that persistently working toward continuous improvement helps us to continually stretch our potential and explore new horizons. We strive to regularly upgrade and fine-tune these methodologies to stay on par with the local and regional business environments. It is this practice of constant self-improvement that helps us keep abreast of the constant change in technology and gives us a competitive edge over others.

Why InDeft

We believe that our success lies in our clients’ success. We strongly adhere to professionalism and have set high standards for services and consultants. A habitude that always brings out the best in our highly skilled team of senior consultants who take charge of each project with diligence and veracity. We focus on understanding our client’s business needs, we comprehend their business-specific language, and in due course of the process, we even live the way they live. By doing so, we help enterprises explore extraordinary opportunities, manage and sustain growth, and maximize revenue.

Our exceptionally skilled, committed, and talented team of consultants, engineers, project managers, and support teams integrate a wide range of technical, business, and financial factors when designing the solution for your business.

Our highly skilled engineering teams have hands-on experience in all aspects of IT solutions, deployment, operational management, and maintenance support. Our world-class architects, design solutions that fit best within your environment and hence, help in achieving your business goals.

Any solution ideally includes the hardware, software, and support resources (team, infrastructure), and documentation to solve a problem or reach a certain business goal. Our Solution architecture starts with a really deep understanding of the business need. Overlooking this aspect is the reason why many projects fail. We spend ample time to gather requirements, envision the solution, evaluate the options available for the solution, perform the time/people/resources trade-offs, design the solution, and then communicate all of this to the developers.
